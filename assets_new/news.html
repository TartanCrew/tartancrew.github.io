Novices' First Visit to the Boathouse

300 WATERFRONT DR, PITTSBURGH, PA (Saturday, September 9, 2017) -

Prospective rowers/coxswains of this year completed their first rundown to our boathouse from skibo gym (~3.8 miles) led by varsity members. Coaches and varsity showed them how to carry boats and oars. Most of the novices had their first time in a boat and felt the seats move on the slides; they learned some new terms about rowing as well.

CAN'T WAIT TO START THEIR FIRST ROW SOON!

First Practice of the New Season

SKIBO GYM, PITTSBURGH, PA (Tuesday, August 29, 2017) -

CMU Rowing started off their first practice in the new erg room. The team completed their first 6k test and it looked like the whole team had put in great work over the summer for this new season! The returning varsity also welcomed five new members who had previous rowing experience to join them for this promising year; they were Jacob Fondriest (Class of 2021), Mabel Bartlett (Class of 2021), Sophie Breedlove (Class of 2021), Isabel Joyce (Class of 2021) and Brenda Mittelbuscher (Graduate Student).

We reached our Crowdfunding goal!!!

PITTSBURGH, PA (Sunday, March 5, 2017) -

Due to amazing support from alumni, parents, fans, and current team members, we have reached our crowdfunding goal of $3500 in about a week! We would like to thank everyone who donated, you generosity is much appreciated!

We raised the funds for our travels to ACRA Henley, which would be our championship race on 5/14-5/15 in Nashville, Tennessee. We have never competed at this race before and are excited to see how competitive we are against new schools! With the funds we also planned to purchase a tent with enclosures and our printed team name and crest. This tent would not only keep us dry during weekend-long races, but would also stand as a symbol for team pride! Check out the crowdfunding website HERE.

2017 Pittsburgh Indoor Sprints

PITTSBURGH, PA (Saturday, January 28, 2017) -

CMU Rowing Club has finished the last event of the day. It's been a very successful competition with seven CMU rowers earning medals and several others with top five finishes. A successful day all around! Now it's back to winter training in preparation for a Camp Bob and the spring season.
