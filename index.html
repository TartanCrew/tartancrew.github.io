Carnegie Mellon University
Rowing Club

Carnegie Mellon University Rowing Club has been rowing on Pittsburgh's rivers for over 30 years!

MISSION STATEMENT

At Carnegie Mellon University Rowing Club, we are committed to fostering an environment where everyone feels welcomed and supported. We believe that this sport has the power to change lives and want to make that opportunity accessible to anyone who wants to join.

ABOUT US

As a team, we are committed to fostering an environment where everyone feels welcomed and supported. Whether you're an undergraduate student, graduate student, faculty, or staff, there's always space on our team for a new member. We welcome anyone who is curious about the sport and is looking to meet new people! (Check out our “Join Us!” page for more information!)

For those new to the sport, we have a novice team that teaches new athletes from the ground up, including skills for water safety. We live by the “no person left behind” motto and are committed to making this sport accessible to anyone who may be interested. For more experienced rowers, and those who have completed their novice year, please join our varsity team!
