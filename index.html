    Welcome!

    This is the website for the Carnegie Mellon University Rowing Crew. Founded in 1987, we've been rowing for a long time something something lazy writing.

    Interested in joining?

    It doesn't matter whether you're an undergraduate or graduate student, there's always space on our team for a new member. We structure our novice programs to teach towards any athletic fitness, and any background. Yes, even if you've never gotten on a boat before.

    Rent-A-Rower

    Rent A Rower is a program that offers people living in the CMU/Shadyside/Squirrel Hill area the ability to "rent" our team members for various needs: cleaning, house work, yard work, moving, whatever!
