2020 Spring Recruitment

Are you a current student at Carnegie Mellon? Join us for the Spring Season now! Rowing is a year-long sport and we welcome new recruits at the start of both the fall semester and the start of the spring semester. Find out more about us here, or come meet us in person. If you're still in high school but are interested in rowing at Carnegie Mellon, please email Hugh Cheon, our recruitment chair, at recruitment@tartancrew.org.

*Please note that we do not recruit as a school varsity sports team. Our recruitment does not involve admission process of the University.

Find Us On Campus

Most of our winter training is in Skibo Gym on CMU campus. We use the erg room for erging practices and currently we also go to the UC gym for weightlifting training.

Find Us at the Boathouse

Our boathouse is located at Three Rivers Association - Washington's Landing. We usually have a team run from campus to the boathouse and have our rowing practices there.
